Before you hire a data scientist or sign a vendor contract, run this assessment. It takes about two hours and will save you months of wasted effort.

## Dimension 1: Data Maturity

AI is only as good as its data. Evaluate:

- **Availability**: Do you have historical data for the problem you want to solve? Minimum 12–24 months for most ML use cases.
- **Quality**: What's your data error rate? > 5% errors will require significant cleaning budget.
- **Accessibility**: Is data siloed across systems? Can you access it programmatically?
- **Labeling**: For supervised learning, do you have labeled examples? Labeling costs are often underestimated by 3-5x.

**Red flag**: "We have lots of data." Everyone says this. What matters is whether the data actually captures the signal you need.

## Dimension 2: Infrastructure

- Can you run ML workloads in your current cloud environment?
- Do you have a data warehouse or data lake?
- What's your CI/CD maturity? AI models need the same rigor as software.

## Dimension 3: Talent

- Do you have data engineers (not just analysts) who can build pipelines?
- Is there an ML engineer who can take a model from notebook to production?
- Do you have a product owner who understands the business use case deeply?

Missing any of these? Plan for it in your roadmap.

## Dimension 4: Organizational Readiness

This is where most assessments stop short. AI requires organizational change:

- Who will own the AI outcome after the project team leaves?
- How will frontline workers interact with the AI output?
- What's your governance process for AI decisions?
- Does leadership understand that AI is a process, not a product?

## Dimension 5: Business Case Clarity

- Can you quantify the value of solving this problem?
- What's the baseline metric today (e.g., fraud rate, churn rate, error rate)?
- What's the minimum improvement needed to justify the investment?
- Is there an executive sponsor who owns the business outcome?

## Scoring Yourself

Rate each dimension 1–5. Under 3 on any dimension = address it before building AI. Under 3 on Data Maturity or Business Case Clarity = stop and fix those first.

A score of 4–5 across all dimensions? You're ready. Let's build something.
